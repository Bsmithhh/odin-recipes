Curry Goat

Curry goat is a rich, flavorful dish popular in Jamaica, seasoned with aromatic spices and slow-cooked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • Goat meat (cut into chunks)
  • Curry Powder
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Thyme
  • Allspice
  • Onion
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Potatoes (optional)
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the Goat Meat:
    • Season the goat meat with curry powder, garlic, ginger, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
    • Mix well and let it marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight in the fridge.
  2. Brown the Meat:
    • Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated goat meat and brown on all sides for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Simmer with Spices:
    • Add chopped onion and Scotch bonnet pepper to the pot.
    • Pour enough water to cover the meat and bring to a boil.
    • Reduce heat to a simmer, cover, and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours until the meat is tender.
  4. Add Potatoes (Optional):
    • Add potatoes to the pot during the last 30 minutes of cooking for a heartier dish.
  5. Final Seasoning:
    • Taste and adjust salt and spices as needed.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve the curry goat with rice or roti, and garnish with fresh thyme if desired.
